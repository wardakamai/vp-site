Our Mission
"We secure the world's most critical energy corridors — so trade can flow without interruption."

Incorporated in the Netherlands, Vopak Holding Terminals B.V. operates as a fully independent entity — holding no equity in crude oil producers or end-buyers. This independence guarantees that our terminal services are delivered with impartiality, making us a trusted counterparty for national oil companies, traders, refineries, and financial institutions alike.

Core Principles
Full operational independence

No upstream or downstream equity interests — pure terminal service provider.

Compliance embedded at every level

ISO, ISPS, OVIQ and local regulatory compliance is non-negotiable across all terminals.

Contractual certainty

Tank allocation agreements with clearly defined tenure, pricing benchmarks, and exit terms.

24/7 operational readiness

Round-the-clock operations, emergency response capability and dedicated client liaison at every site.

Our History

Four Decades of Reliability

Built on decades of operational excellence at some of the most demanding energy hubs in the world.

How We Work

Our Operational Approach

From initial request to ongoing operations, every step is designed to give clients certainty, visibility and control.

1 — Commercial Structuring

We begin every engagement by understanding your product, volume, tenure, and delivery requirements. Our commercial team structures bespoke spot or term agreements aligned to your operational and financial objectives.

2 — Tank Allocation & Inspection

Upon agreement, dedicated tank(s) are allocated and independently inspected. Pre-receipt, in-transit and discharge surveys are performed by accredited third-party inspectors to ensure product quality and quantity integrity.

3 — Logistics & Vessel Scheduling

Our operations team coordinates vessel nominations, berth scheduling, customs documentation and port authority liaisons at all four terminals. We support FOB, CIF, DAP and custom Incoterms arrangements.

4 — Compliance & Documentation

End-to-end documentation management — bills of lading, certificates of origin, quality certificates, customs declarations and AML/KYC counterparty verification — delivered to global regulatory standards.
