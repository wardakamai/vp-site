Terminal Services

Every Vopak Holding terminal offers a standardised suite of storage and logistics services, with site-specific capabilities available on request.

+
01
Dedicated Tank Allocation

Contractually guaranteed dedicated tank capacity. No shared-tank risk, no allocation conflicts. Your product, your tank, your terms — for spot or term periods.

+
02
Throughput & Blending

Full throughput services including loading, discharge, product blending and quality adjustment. In-tank and in-line blending available at Rotterdam and Fujairah terminals.

+
03
Multimodal Connectivity

Vessel, pipeline, rail and road connectivity at all terminals. FOB, CIF and DAP delivery terms supported with full documentation and chartering coordination.

+
04
Quality Control & Inspection

Independent cargo inspection, API gravity testing, water and sediment analysis, and full chain-of-custody documentation from loading to discharge at every terminal.

+
05
24/7 Terminal Access

Round-the-clock terminal operations with real-time inventory monitoring, dedicated liaison officers, and emergency response protocols available at every facility globally.

+
06
Compliance Documentation

Full regulatory documentation including bill of lading, certificate of quality, cargo manifests and customs clearance support across all terminal jurisdictions.
