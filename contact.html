FAQ

Common Questions

How quickly can storage be arranged?

For spot requirements, we can often confirm tank availability within 24 hours. Term agreements typically require 3–5 business days for documentation and compliance checks.

What is the minimum storage volume?

Minimum storage volumes vary by terminal and product. Typically 25,000 bbls for crude oil, 10,000 bbls for petroleum products. Contact us for specific requirements.

Do you offer product blending services?

Yes. Crude oil blending and petroleum product blending is available at Rotterdam and Fujairah. Blending is performed to client specification with full independent inspection.

Are independent inspectors permitted on-site?

Absolutely. We actively encourage independent cargo inspection by accredited surveyors (SGS, Intertek, Bureau Veritas) at all stages. Access is coordinated through our operations team.

Independent terminal storage for crude oil, petroleum products, LNG and chemicals at four global hubs.
