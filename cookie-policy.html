Cookie Policy

Last updated: 14 May 2026

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites function correctly, to remember your preferences, and to provide information to site owners. This policy explains how Vopak Holding Terminals B.V. uses cookies on www.vpterminalholding.com.

Cookies We Use

This website uses only strictly necessary (essential) cookies. No analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking cookies are set.

Cookie name Duration Purpose
__session Session Maintains your session state while navigating the site. Deleted when you close your browser.
__cf_bm 30 minutes Set by Cloudflare to distinguish between human visitors and automated bots, protecting the site from malicious traffic.

Cookies We Do Not Use

We do not use any of the following:

  • Analytics cookies (e.g. Google Analytics, Matomo)
  • Advertising or retargeting cookies
  • Social media tracking pixels
  • Third-party profiling or behavioural tracking

Managing Cookies

Because we use only essential cookies, disabling them may affect the basic functionality of this website. You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome — Settings › Privacy and security › Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox — Settings › Privacy & Security › Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari — Preferences › Privacy › Manage Website Data
  • Edge — Settings › Cookies and site permissions

Contact

If you have questions about our use of cookies or this policy, please contact us at contact@vpterminalholding.com. For broader privacy matters, see our Privacy Policy.
