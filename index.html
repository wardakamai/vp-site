Vopak Holding Terminals B.V. is an independent terminal storage operator specialising in the global movement of crude oil, petroleum products, LNG, and chemical feedstocks. Operating at the intersection of trade, logistics, and risk management, we connect producers with end buyers across the most critical energy corridors worldwide.

With facilities at Rotterdam's Botlek/Europoort complex, Houston Ship Channel, Fujairah's UAE East Coast hub, and Singapore's Jurong Island, we provide guaranteed tank allocation, compliance-first operations, and 24/7 terminal access.
